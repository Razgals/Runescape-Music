About

Sounds of RuneScape

The gameplay usually gets all the nostalgia, but the music was just as much a part of it. And it's the one thing that can really take you back in time, if you were there to hear it the first time.

So I wanted to give it a proper place. All the tracks, every version, the way they used to sound.
